Rock Papper Scissors

Welcome to a glorious game of rock papper scissors, you will be playing against the computer to finally find out who has the brightest mind, Is it man or machine!!!

The rules are simple you will be playing up to five rounds with 1 point per round which will be displayed in the result section. After picking your gesture the make your choice button will become available so that you can run it up against the computer. Then winner of the round will recieve one point untill one player has three points and will be decleared winner!
